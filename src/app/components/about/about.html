About Me

I'm Muhammad Shahadat Hossain, a software engineer from Bangladesh with a deep-rooted passion for building things that solve real world problems. During my graduation period at SUST, my journey began with solving algorithmic problems that taught me how to think before I code.

Over the past 4 years at Streams Tech Ltd, I've built enterprise-grade systems for organizations like Bangladesh Railway and CARE Bangladesh — applications that institutions depend on every day. From web platforms managing railway infrastructure to cross-platform desktop software for storm water modeling, I've shipped code that operates at scale.

I believe in clean architecture, writing maintainable code coping with the pace of business, and having endless curiosity to learn new things. Always ready to take on the next challenge that pushes boundaries.
